House Inventorying

Have you ever had something but just can't remember where you stashed it? Well, if you start using this house-inventorying app, you won't need to worry about it. After spending hours of inventorying of your items (who knew your job at the department store would pay off) you can now have all items locations at your fingertips!

NOTE: This site is only in its alpha stages. So if you find any problems or have any questions please contact me. or comment on my repo:

Feature Notes

Working Features:

  • Creating of a House
  • Inventorying of your items in your house
  • Searching for locations of items
  • Searching for locations of types of items

Upcoming Features:

  • Logging in
  • 2D Rendering of floors (Map View)
  • Sharing your house with another user
  • Searching items will highlight their location on a map where it should be located
